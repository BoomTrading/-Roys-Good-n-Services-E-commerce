What options do I have?

To properly handle this situation, consider these alternatives:

  • Archive the booking data for record-keeping purposes
Quick Tip

For financial and auditing purposes, it's often better to keep records of bookings and payments rather than deleting them. Consider implementing a status field to mark bookings as "cancelled" or "inactive" instead.
